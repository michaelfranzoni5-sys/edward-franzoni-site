Edward M. Franzoni, Ph.D.

Licensed Psychologist — Over 30 Years of Experience

Dr. Edward Franzoni is an Ivy League educated (B.S., Cornell University; M.S., Columbia University), licensed psychologist with over 30 years of experience. He received his Ph.D. from Fordham University, trained at Monmouth Medical Center in Long Branch, N.J., and served as Clinical Director at Kimball Medical Center in Lakewood, N.J. (now RWJ Barnabas Health), prior to entering private practice.

His excellent training, coupled with his many years of experience, enable him to competently and effectively address a wide range of needs and provide the following services:

Frequently Asked Questions

What insurance does Dr. Franzoni accept?
Dr. Franzoni accepts Horizon BC/BS, Medicare, Cigna, United Behavioral Health, United Health Care, Oxford, Optum, UMR, all patients with out-of-network benefits, and all self-pay patients. Some services may not be covered by insurance — contact the office to confirm.
What is sports psychology and who is it for?
Sports psychology focuses on the mental and emotional aspects of athletic performance. Dr. Franzoni works with young athletes and their families to address the unique challenges they face, including performance anxiety, injury recovery, and return-to-sport psychology.
Does Dr. Franzoni see children and adolescents?
Yes. Dr. Franzoni treats children, adolescents, and adults. He provides individual, couples/marital, and family counseling, and has extensive experience working with young athletes and their families.
Where is Dr. Franzoni's office located?
Dr. Franzoni's office is located at 3820 River Rd, Point Pleasant, NJ 08742. He serves patients throughout Ocean County including Point Pleasant Beach, Brick, Toms River, Lakewood, Howell, and surrounding areas.
Does Dr. Franzoni offer forensic and custody evaluations?
Yes. Dr. Franzoni provides court-ordered and non-court-ordered forensic evaluations, brief custody assessments, parenting coordination, co-parent counseling, and consultation for child custody and related matters.
