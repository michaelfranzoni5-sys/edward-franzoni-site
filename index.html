Edward M. Franzoni, Ph.D.

Licensed Psychologist

Dr. Edward Franzoni is an Ivy League educated, (Bachelor of Science, Cornell University, Master's Degree, Columbia University), licensed psychologist with over 30 years of experience. Dr. Franzoni, who received his Ph.D. from Fordham University, trained at Monmouth Medical Center in Long Branch, N.J. and also served as Clinical Director at Kimball Medical Center, (both are now part of RWJ/Barnabas Health), prior to entering into private practice. His excellent training, coupled with his many years of experience, enable him to effectively address the issues and problems listed below, as well as others. Dr. Franzoni will gladly answer any questions that you may have prior to scheduling.
